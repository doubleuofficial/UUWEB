The Artist Story

Bridging Kentucky 270 Roots with Oklahoma City 405 Urban Noir

Originally from Hopkinsville, Kentucky, hip-hop artist DoubleU established his architectural foundation for musical layout during multi-year marching and concert band compositions.

From serving as a U.S. Army Combat Medic (68W) managing high-intensity trauma scenarios to working through intense real-world personal and legal trials, his tracks operate as a permanent audio vault. No forced commercial formulas—just pure, transparent documentation of real-world survival and resilience.
